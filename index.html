    Best Practices

    • Use Strong Passwords: Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
    • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.
    • Regularly Update Passwords: Change your passwords regularly to reduce the risk of compromise.
    • Avoid Reusing Passwords: Use a different password for each of your accounts.
    • Use a Password Manager: Store and manage your passwords securely.
